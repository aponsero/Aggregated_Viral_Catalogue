Common AVrC subsets

Data subsets including only the fasta sequence :


  • Representative dataset: Subset of all 449,859 vOTU representative sequences of the AVrC

  • Complete dataset: The complete dataset of 1,018,941 unique sequences


Data subsets including fasta sequence and accompanying summary metadata csv file:


  • High quality dataset: Subset of 130,092 vOTU representative sequences classified as "Complete" or "High Quality" by CheckV

  • Bacteriophage only dataset: Subset of 380,747 representative vOTU sequences classified as belonging to the "Uroviricota", "Phixviricota", "Hofneiviricota" phyla by GeNomad
